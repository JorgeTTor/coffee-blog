Tipos de granos de café

Not all coffee beans are created equal! The four main varieties – Arabica, Robusta, Liberica, and Excelsa – each boast unique flavor profiles. Arabica, the most popular, is known for its smooth, balanced taste with hints of fruit and sweetness. Robusta offers a stronger cup with a bit of bitterness and nuttiness. Liberica is rarer and brings a woody, smoky character, while Excelsa is known for its complex, fruity, and dark chocolate notes. Understanding these differences can help you explore the exciting world of coffee and find your perfect cup.
